## Design Context

### Users
Internal team members generating high-quality model/product photos for Clutches & More listings. They want speed, clarity, and confidence that the output is “ready to post” without babysitting the workflow.

### Brand Personality
Playful, modern, energetic — but still trustworthy and polished (not toy-like). The UI should feel like a friendly studio tool with a premium finish.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme “studio daylight” interface: bright canvas, soft surfaces, crisp type, playful accent color, and subtle depth. Prioritize readability, quick scanning, and reassuring status feedback during long-running generations.

### Design Principles
- Reduce cognitive load: one primary action per screen, obvious next step.
- Make state visible: clear progress, empty/error states that guide recovery.
- Use playful accents sparingly: surfaces stay calm; accents earn attention.
- Sweat the details: consistent spacing scale, focus rings, and motion.
